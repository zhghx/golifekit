Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GoLifeKit ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website and purchase our products. By using GoLifeKit, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver our product and support you:

  • Communications: If you email us, we keep your messages and contact details to respond and improve our service.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous analytics such as pages visited and general location to understand how visitors use our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your purchase and provide access to the product.
  • To send you setup instructions, receipts, and important product updates.
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To improve our website and products.

3. Payment Processing

We do not store your full payment card details. All payments are handled by trusted third-party processors (such as Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, or Stripe), who maintain their own security and privacy standards. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our site may use cookies and privacy-friendly analytics to measure traffic and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time; some features may not work as intended if you do.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with service providers who help us operate our business (such as payment processors and email tools), and only to the extent necessary to provide our service, or when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our service and meet legal or accounting obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below).

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@golifekit.com.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

GoLifeKit is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@golifekit.com.
